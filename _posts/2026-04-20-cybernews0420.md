## Cyber Attacks Fuel Surge in Cargo Theft Across Logistics Industry
<span class="badge badge-primary" style="font-size: 0.7rem;">Threat Intelligence</span>

This convergence of cyber and physical crime is creating new risks for supply chains worldwide. Organizations in the logistics sector are urged to strengthen cybersecurity controls and improve visibility across operations.

## Tycoon 2FA Hackers Exploit Device Code Phishing
<span class="badge badge-primary" style="font-size: 0.7rem;">Phishing</span>

By exploiting legitimate authentication workflows, attackers trick users into granting access without exposing passwords. Organizations should restrict device code flows and monitor authentication activity closely.
