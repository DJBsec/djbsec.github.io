Background

I started in IT at the helpdesk and worked my way through systems administration, network engineering, and eventually senior leadership — touching nearly every layer of enterprise infrastructure along the way.

In recent years my focus has shifted fully to cybersecurity. I’ve led the design and build of a security program from the ground up, balancing hands-on technical work with strategy, compliance, and risk management. I hold certifications from Cisco and Palo Alto Networks, and earned my CISSP in 2024.
